AI BENCHMARK APPLICATION:   PRIVACY POLICY

PERMISSIONS REQUIRED:

- android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- android.permission.INTERNET

Neural networks used in the benchmark are zipped into a single archive to reduce the size of the application. The first two permissions are needed to unpack them to phone's storage and then access from the application. The last permission is needed for Google Firebase that is used for collecting benchmark results and device technical specifications.

BENCHMARK RESULTS:

When you use our benchmarking software, we collect information about the hardware and software specifications of your mobile device together with the final benchmark scores. This system information does not contain any personally identifiable information about you.

The application uses Google Firebase that additionally provides simple information about the normal user interaction with the application, e.g. openned screens, application crashes and similar events. No personal data is collected.
