Location:   ETH (virtual, hosted by ETH)

Dates:   4th of March 2022 (1 day event)

Participation:   Invitation only event

Participants:   Maximum 20 to 30 participants

Chairs:   Andrey Ignatov, Radu Timofte and Emilia Tantar

Context & Scope:   Given the tremendous advances in Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence from the recent years, the industrial uptake requires a solid foundational basis for comparison of different hardware and software based solutions designed for running various Machine Learning and AI workloads. The workshop focuses on the challenges raised by AI-benchmarking and ways to support the research community in tackling the various aspects of AI-testing and benchmarking. Some aspects to be potentially covered are:

●  AI performance evaluation on edge devices
●  Efficient AI testing for cloud infrastructures
●  AI powered test automation
●  AI benchmarking for maintenance
●  Machine and deep learning frameworks for mobile devices
●  Real-time image segmentation and benchmarking
●  Optimized NLP-testing and multi-lingual support
●  Industry-driven applications related to the above problems

AI and machine learning based solutions for mobile, embedded and IoT platforms fall under this scope. The topics of interest cover all major aspects of AI and deep learning testing and benchmarking research for mobile devices, edge devices and cloud environments. To ensure high quality of the exchanges, participation is by invitation only and targets research and industry experts from the corresponding fields. The authors will be invited to present their work during the first part of the actual workshop event. The second part will be reserved for exchanges inside the 3 selected topic areas and aim for a close collaboration inside European project proposals.
